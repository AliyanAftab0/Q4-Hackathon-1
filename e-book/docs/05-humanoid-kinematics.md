# Humanoid Kinematics

Understanding how to control a bipedal robot.

## Forward vs Inverse Kinematics

- **FK**: Joint angles -> End effector position.
- **IK**: End effector position -> Joint angles.

## ZMP & Walking

The **Zero Moment Point (ZMP)** is crucial for stability in bipedal walking.
